CodeWorks BlackJack Game

Rules

Official rules can be found here. The following rules were implemented here:

  • Player is dealt two cards to start. Dealer is then also dealt two cards with only first one visible.
  • Ace can be worth one or eleven. Jack, Queen and King are worth ten.
  • Beat the dealer with 21 or having more points while under 21.
  • "Bust" is when you exceed 21.

How to Play

Deal Cards to see your starting point. Hit Me to receive another card. Stand when you are finished to see dealers card.
